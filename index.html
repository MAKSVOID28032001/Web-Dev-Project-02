About QuizQuest

QuizQuest is an engaging quiz app project designed to enhance knowledge and foster learning through interactive quizzes. Developed using a dynamic blend of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, the app offers a seamless user experience with its intuitive interface and responsive design. Users can test their knowledge across various subjects, reinforcing learning through a gamified approach. The combination of these web technologies allows for a visually appealing and functional quiz game, providing a fun and educational platform for users to challenge themselves and improve their understanding of diverse topics.
